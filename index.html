OOPs concept

Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) is a programming paradigm based on the concept of objects and classes, promoting modularity, reusability, and maintainability. The four key principles of OOP are Encapsulation (hiding data and restricting direct access), Abstraction, Inheritance and Polymorphism (enabling multiple forms of a method or function, such as method overloading and overriding).
These principles help in designing efficient, scalable, and organized code in languages like Java, C++, and Python.
